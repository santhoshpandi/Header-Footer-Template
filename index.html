Desert

Deserts are the driest places on Earth, with some characteristics.

Vegetation

Deserts have sparse vegetation, with trees usually absent. Plants that do grow in deserts are often tough and wiry, with small or no leaves, water-resistant cuticles, and sometimes spines.
